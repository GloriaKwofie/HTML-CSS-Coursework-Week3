Welcome

Cherry Cakes is one of the best bakeries in the world. This bakery was started by Mr & Mrs Kwofie in the 1980's. Cherry Bakery specializes in cakes even though we offer other pastries. Our cakes are hand-made using traditional recipes and organic products. Some of the cakes we bake are Butter Cake, Pound Cake, Sponge Cake, Genoise Cake, Biscuit Cake and Chiffon Cake.

The bakery is opened on Monday-Sunday from 8am-6pm. We are also opened for special orders which are not in this time frame. You can order your cake for any event and it will be delivered to your satisfaction. We do orders for:

  • WEDDINGS
  • BIRTHDAYS
  • GRADUATIONS
  • NAMING CEREMONY
  • CHRISTMAS
  • ANY OTHER EVENT
